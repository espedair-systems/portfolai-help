The **Visualizer Hub** (Image Viewer) is a dedicated environment for deep inspection and review of your visual assets. It moves beyond simple browsing, providing the full resolution and technical context of every image in your library.

## <SketchUnderline>Precision Viewing</SketchUnderline>

The visualizer is designed for high-performance rendering of high-resolution files.

### Navigation Controls
- **Next/Previous**: Use the on-screen chevron overlays or your **Arrow Keys** (← / →) to step through your current collection.
- **Autoplay**: Toggle the **Autoplay** (Slideshow) mode to sit back while PortfolAI cycles through your assets every 4 seconds. You can also tap the **Spacebar** to toggle playback.
- **Zoom**: Click on the image to activate the high-fidelity zoom mode for detail inspection.

---

## <SketchUnderline>Metadata & Inspection</SketchUnderline>

The right-hand sidebar provides a deep dive into the asset's technical and descriptive data.

### Technical Details
- **Resolution**: High-precision width and height data.
- **Format**: File encapsulation types (PNG, JPEG, WebP, etc.).
- **Timestamps**: Exactly when the asset was ingested and last modified.

### Descriptive Layer
- **Description**: Detailed context provided during ingestion or generated by AI.
- **Taxonomy Tokens**: A list of tagged metadata associated with the asset.

---

## <SketchUnderline>Integrated Workflow</SketchUnderline>

The Visualizer is not just for viewing; it's a launchpad for further refinement.
- **Rating**: Set your preference level for an asset directly from the viewer.
- **Editor Access**: Click the **Editor** button to jump directly into the Canvas for creative adjustments.
- **Back to Archive**: Use the top-left return arrow to quickly jump back to your previous grid position.
