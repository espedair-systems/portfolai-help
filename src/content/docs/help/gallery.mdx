The **Gallery** is where your images come to life. It's more than just a folder; it's a fluid, beautiful space designed to showcase your work in the best possible light.

## <SketchUnderline>Seeing Your Work</SketchUnderline>

The gallery uses a "Masonry" layout—this means images of all different shapes and sizes fit together perfectly without being cropped or stretched.

- **Expand**: Click any image to open it in full-screen **Detail View**.
- **Zoom**: Use your mouse wheel or pinch-to-zoom to see every detail of your high-resolution files.
- **Info**: Hover over an image to see its name, or look at the sidebar in Detail View for technical specs.

## <SketchUnderline>Organizing Your Favorites</SketchUnderline>

### Selection Mode
To manage many images at once, just **long-press** or click the tiny circle on any image. Once in Selection Mode, you can:
- **Bulk Tag**: Add or remove labels from dozens of images at once.
- **Archive**: Move older projects out of sight but keep them safe.
- **Feature**: Pick your best shots to appear in the "Top Picks" showcase.
