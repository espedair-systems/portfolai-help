Welcome to your PortfolAI dashboard! We've designed the interface to feel like a digital sketchbook—simple to use, yet capable of organizing your most complex ideas and collections.

## <SketchUnderline>Where is Everything?</SketchUnderline>

The top menu is your primary way to jump between sections:

- **Home**: Your central hub. See your most recent imports and highlights.
- **Search**: Find exactly what you're looking for using keywords or AI tags.
- **Gallery**: Your beautiful, full-screen image showcase.
- **Process Manager**: Check on the progress of AI tasks and system performance.
- **Theme**: Swap between Light and Dark modes to suit your mood.
- **Help**: You're here! Access guides and tutorials at any time.
- **Settings**: Manage your library paths and system preferences.

---

## <SketchUnderline>Quick Tips</SketchUnderline>

### Getting Help Contextually
Look for the **(?) icon** next to settings or section titles. It opens a quick "Deep Dive" modal with explanations and links for more detail.

### Supercharged Navigation
Want to move even faster? Press `Cmd+K` (or `Ctrl+K`) to open the **Command Palette**. Type where you want to go, and hit Enter.

---

## <SketchUnderline>View on Any Device</SketchUnderline>

PortfolAI follows you everywhere. The dashboard automatically resizes whether you are on a massive workstation monitor or checking your gallery from your phone.
