The **Image Library** is your specialized view for all still media assets. Whether you're working with photography, digital art, or screenshots, this section provides the tools you need to browse and organize them efficiently.

## <SketchUnderline>Library Features</SketchUnderline>

Unlike the general dashboard, the Image Library is tuned for high-density visual scanning of still assets.

### Filtering Your View
Use the filter dropdown at the top of the library to narrow down your collection:
- **Default Filter**: Shows all images that meet your system's global minimum rating.
- **Project Specific**: View images grouped by specific AI-tagged projects or folder structures.
- **Quality Gates**: Filter by rating to see only your best work (e.g., 4+ stars).

---

## <SketchUnderline>Navigation & Controls</SketchUnderline>

### Sorting Options
Stay organized by switching your library view:
- **Newest/Oldest**: Sort by the date the image was added to your library.
- **Top Rated**: Bring your most successful pieces to the front.
- **Name**: Standard alphabetical sorting by filename.

### Keyboard Shortcuts
- **Arrow Keys**: Navigate between thumbnails in the grid.
- **Enter**: Open the selected image in the **Media Player**.
- **1-5**: Quickly assign a rating to the selected image.

---

## <SketchUnderline>Media Detail View</SketchUnderline>

Clicking any image will open it in the full-screen player. From there, you can:
- **Inspect Metadata**: See dimensions, file type, and AI-generated description.
- **Manage Tags**: Add or remove labels to help the AI categorize your work better.
- **Deduplicate**: If PortfolAI finds a similar image, it will be flagged in the sidebar for easy cleanup.
