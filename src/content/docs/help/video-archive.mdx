The **Video Archive** is where your motion projects live. Optimized for video asset management, this section provides specialized metadata and playback controls for your cinematic collection.

## <SketchUnderline>Archive Features</SketchUnderline>

The Video Archive extends the standard library capabilities with motion-specific tools.

### Technical Metadata
Hovering over a video thumbnail or viewing it in the player provides:
- **Duration**: The total length of the asset.
- **Resolution**: High-definition markers (4K, 1080p, etc.).
- **Codec Info**: Technical details about the video encryption for expert users.

---

## <SketchUnderline>Playback & Organization</SketchUnderline>

### Resume Watch
PortfolAI automatically remembers your position in any video. If you step away and return later, the system will offer to resume playback from exactly where you left off.

### Sorting & Filtering
- **Duration**: Sort by length to find everything from quick clips to feature-length projects.
- **Recent**: See what you've processed or watched most recently.
- **Video Tags**: Filter by AI-detected motion content like "Aerial", "Slow Motion", or "Action".

---

## <SketchUnderline>Managing Large Files</SketchUnderline>

Videos take up significantly more space than images. Use the archive tools to keep your library lean:
- **Proxy Management**: PortfolAI creates small, fast-loading "proxy" versions of your videos for smooth browsing.
- **Cleanup Suggestions**: The AI will flag duplicate video assets or extremely low-resolution clips for potential archiving or removal.
