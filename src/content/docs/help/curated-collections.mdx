**Curated Collections** are dynamic, filterable views of your library that allow you to focus on specific themes, projects, or high-value assets. Unlike static folders, collections are powered by real-time metadata and AI categorization.

## <SketchUnderline>Advanced Filtering</SketchUnderline>

The collections interface provides powerful tools for surgical precision in finding assets.

### Media Selection
Quickly toggle between **All Assets**, **Images Only**, or **Videos Only** using the sidebar controls. The entry counts update instantly as you change your parameters.

### Taxonomy & Tagging
Use the **Categorization** dropdown to filter by specific AI-detected labels. 
- You can select multiple labels to create complex "AND" queries.
- The **Empty Taxonomy** marker indicates no labels match your current filter string.
- Use **Reset All Labels** to quickly clear your categorization stack.

---

## <SketchUnderline>Saving Your Workflow</SketchUnderline>

If you find yourself frequently using the same combination of filters and sorting, you can save it as a permanent **Saved Filter**.

1. Configure your desired Type, Display Order, and Labels.
2. Click the **Save** button in the top header.
3. Name your filter (e.g., *"Top Action Clips"* or *"2024 Landscape Shots"*).
4. Access it anytime via the **Saved Filters** dropdown in the sidebar.

---

## <SketchUnderline>Smart Rankings</SketchUnderline>

PortfolAI uses engagement patterns and quality markers to prioritize assets:
- **Top Rated**: Prioritizes items you've marked with higher ratings.
- **Newest First**: The default view for fresh captures.
- **Metadata Clusters**: The system automatically groups assets that share high temporal or visual similarity.
